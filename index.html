Our Story

About Tivmark

Tivmark Advisory was founded in 2016 with a straightforward idea: early-stage founders deserve the same caliber of financial guidance that typically only Series C+ companies can afford.

After eight years at a Big Four firm specializing in emerging tech companies, our founder saw the gap firsthand. Startups were either overpaying for enterprise-level firms that didn't prioritize them, or cobbling together freelance bookkeepers and tax preparers with no strategic thread connecting the work.

Today, Tivmark serves roughly 90 active clients across the Bay Area, primarily seed-to-Series B startups alongside a handful of established professional firms. The practice has grown entirely through referrals, which we take as the strongest possible signal that the model works.
